About

Hi.

My name is Annie Kelly. I am working towards a PhD in Technology and Media at the University of Colorado Boulder, where I also earned my BS in Computer Science. I have been dubbed the "code witch" by people in my life because of my passion for figuring out how to build all sorts of weird interactive things. Programming, electronics, design, and fabrication are my specialties and I am always trying to learn new skills.

Feel free to contact me if you'd like to discuss contract work opportunies for any crazy original ideas you'd like to see come to life!
